App Name: UniClinic
Purpose: Book doctor appointments online.
 
Features:
- Create account using email + password.
- Login using email + password.
- Book an appointment by choosing: doctor, date, time slot.
- Cancel appointment up to 2 hours before the appointment.
 
Support:
- Support hours: 9 AM to 5 PM (Sunday to Thursday).
- Contact email: support@uniclinic.com
 
Pricing:
- Booking is free.
- Doctors set their own consultation fees.
 
Platforms:
- Android only.
- iOS is not available yet.
