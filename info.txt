App Name: Smart Student Advisor (SSA)
Purpose: deliver the sauitable subjects for the university student at Faculty of Engineering - Mansoura university

Description: Smart Student Advisor (SSA) helps Engineering students at Mansoura University select suitable subjects based on their academic level, prerequisites, and university regulations. It provides clear recommendations and guidance to support better course planning each semester.
How to Use (Web): Create an account, log in, enter your academic level and completed subjects, then review the recommended subjects and save your semester plan.
